Superscript and Subscript

  • Superscript is used to display text that is smaller than the normal text and is raised above the baseline.
  • Subscript is used to display text that is smaller than the normal text and is lowered below the baseline.
  • Both superscript and subscript are often used in mathematical and scientific formulas.
